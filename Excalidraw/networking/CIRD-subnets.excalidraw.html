      Text Elements §

      192.168.11.0/26

      11111111.11111111.11111111.11000000

      26 bits are 1’s from left-right

      convert back to octets

      11111111 = 255 11000000 = 192

      255.255.255.192

      255-192 + 1 (for 0) = 64 IP’s in range

      64 - 1 (broadcast IP) - 1 (network IP) = 62 IP’s available

      192.168.11.0 Network 192.168.11.63 Broadcast

      192.168.11.1-62 available
